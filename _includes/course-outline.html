How to be Successful
  • Be punctual and attend regularly.
  • Maintain complete and neat notes.
  • Ask questions, seek assistance, and be an active participant in class.
  • Complete homework and assignments - practice makes perfect.
  • Study thoroughly for exams. Be sure to complete all assigned questions, reviews and practice exams.
  • Get assistance immediately when you encounter problems.
Attendance
  • Consistent and regular attendance is key to success in any course.
  • If you do have to miss class, you are expected to notify your teacher by email ahead of time.
  • It is your responsibility to get caught up on all missed material before the next class.
