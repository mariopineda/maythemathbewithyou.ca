Evaluate without a calculator:

  1. $$ 3-(-5) $$
  2. $$ -12 \div (-4) $$
  3. $$ -5 + (-4) $$
  4. $$ 12(-2) $$
  5. $$ (-14)(-0.5) $$
  6. $$ -2+3 \times 5 $$
  7. $$ (-3)(4)-(-7) $$
  8. $$ (-4+1)-(-2 \times 5) $$
{% include solution-start.html %}

A.

$$ 3-(-5) = 3+5 = 8 $$

B.

$$ -12 \div (-4) = \frac{\displaystyle -12}{\displaystyle -4} = \frac{\displaystyle 12}{\displaystyle 4} = 3 $$

C.

$$ -5 + (-4) = -5-4 = -9 $$

D.

$$ 12(-2) = -24 $$

E.

$$ (-14)(-0.5) = 14 \times 0.5 = 7 $$

E.

$$ -2+3 \times 5 = -2 + 15 = 13 $$

F.

$$ (-3)(4)-(-7) = -12 + 7 = -5 $$

G.

$$ (-4+1)-(-2 \times 5) = -3-(-10)=-3+10=7 $$ {% include solution-end.html %}
