As an athlete on a plant-based diet you are concerned about your protein intake. Your coach has instructed you that your post-workout meal has to contain 1 soy-based source of protein, 2 different legume-based proteins, and three different nut-based sources of protein. Determine the number of possible post-workout meals that can be made from 2 different soy-based dishes, 5 different legume dishes and 9 different nut varieties.
