As an athlete on a plant-based diet you need to ensure an adequate protein intake. Your coach has instructed you that your post-workout meal has to contain 1 soy-based source of protein, 2 different legume-based proteins, and three different nut/seed-based sources of protein. Determine the number of possible post-workout meals that can be made from the following food items:

  • Soy-based sources of protein:
    • tempeh
    • tofu
  • Legume-based sources of protein:
    • dal
    • black beans
    • hummus
    • kidney beans
    • black lentils
  • Nut/seed-bases sources of protein:
    • walnuts
    • cashews
    • chia seeds
    • hemp hearts
    • flax seeds
    • tahini paste
    • pistachios
    • pumpkin seeds
    • sunflower seeds
{% if true %}

The order of the different dishes does not matter. $\binom{2}{1} \times \binom{5}{2} \times \binom{9}{3} = 1690$ different post-workout meals.
