Evaluate without a calculator:

  1. $$ (10+2-5) \times (6^2 \div (8-4)) $$
  2. $$ 10+8-6^2 \div (3^2 \times 4) $$
  3. $$ 8 \div (10-9)^3 \times 7 + 4^2 $$
  4. $$ (10 \times (6+4)) \div (2^3-7)^2 $$
{% include solution-start.html %} Evaluate using BEDMAS order (brackets, exponents, division and multiplication, addition and subtraction).

A.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} & & (10+2-5) \times (6^2 \div (8-4)) \\ & = & 7 \times 36 \div 4 \\ & = & 7 \times 9 \\ & = & 63 \end{array} $$

B.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} & & 10+8-6^2 \div (3^2 \times 4) \\ & = & 10+8-36 \div (p \times 4) \\ & = & -18 \div 36 \\ & = & \frac{\displaystyle -18}{\displaystyle 36} = -\frac{\displaystyle 1}{\displaystyle 2} \end{array} $$ {% include solution-end.html %}
