Simplify and express in descending order of power:

  1. $$ (3-a-2a^2)+(9-4a+5a^2) $$
  2. $$ (5x^2-8x+3)-(3x^3-3x-1) $$
  3. Subtract the sum of $ 2x^3-7x^2-6x+1 $ and $ 8-3x+5x^2-4x^3 $ from $ 2x^3-7x+9 $.

{% if true %}

A.

$(3-a-2a^2)+(9-4a+5a^2) = 3-a-2a^2+9-4a+5a^2 = 3a^2-5a+12$

B.

$(5x^2-8x+3)-(3x^3-3x-1) = 5x^2-8x+3-3x^3+3x+1 = -3x^3+5x^2-5x+4$

C.

\[ \begin{align} & (2x^3-7x+9)-[(2x^3-7x^2-6x+1)+(8-3x+5x^2-4x^3)] \\ = & (2x^3-7x+9)-(2x^3-7x^2-6x+1+8-3x+5x^2-4x^3)\\ = & 2x^3-7x+9-2x^3+7x^2+6x-1-8+3x-5x^2+4x^3 \\ = & 4x^3+2x^2+2x \end{align} \]
