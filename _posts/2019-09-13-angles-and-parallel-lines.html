Determine the degree measure of the angles marked by letters.

Order of angles reflect one possible order in which they can be determined.
  • Determine $d$: $d+35^{\circ}+60^{\circ}=180^{\circ} \Rightarrow d=85^{\circ}$ (Alternate interior angles)
  • Determine $e$: $e=60^{\circ}$ (Alternate interior angles)
  • Determine $g$: $g=e$ (Opposite angles)
  • Determine $f$: $e+f=180 \Rightarrow f=120^{\circ}$ (Straight line angle)
  • Determine $j$: $j=e=60^{\circ}$ (Corresponding angle)
  • Determine $h$: $h=68^{\circ}$ (Corresponding angle)
  • Determine $i$: $h+i+j=180^{\circ} \Rightarrow i=52^{\circ}$ (Straight line angles)
  • Determine $k$: $k+68^{\circ}=180^{\circ} \Rightarrow k=112^{\circ}$ (Straight line angles)
  • Determine $l$: $l=68^{\circ}$ (Opposite angles)
  • Determine $c$: $c=k=112^{\circ}$ (Opposite angles)
  • Determine $a$: $a=35^{\circ}$ (Alternate interior angles)
  • Determine $b$: $a+b+c=180^{\circ} \Rightarrow b=33^{\circ}$ (Same side interior angles)
