Determine the degree measure of the angles marked by letters.

Order of angles reflect one possible order in which they can be determined.
  • $d+35^{\circ}+60^{\circ}=180^{\circ} \Rightarrow d=85^{\circ}$ (Alternate interior angles)
  • $e+d+35^{\circ}=180^{\circ} \Rightarrow 60^{\circ}$ where $d=85^{\circ}$ (Straight line angle)
