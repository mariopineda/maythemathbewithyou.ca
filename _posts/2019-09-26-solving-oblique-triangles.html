Solve $\triangle ABC$ where $AD \bot BC$, $\angle BAD = 20^{\circ}$, $\angle CAD = 55^{\circ}$, $\angle DCA=35^{\circ}$ and $c=12.5$ cm. Round angles to the nearest degree and sides to nearest tenth.

{% if true %}

One possible way of solving $\triangle ABC$.

Find $\angle ABD$: $\angle ABD = 180^{\circ}-35^{\circ}-55^{\circ}-20^{\circ} = 70^{\circ}$ (Triangle Sum Theorem)

Find $\angle BAC$: $\angle BAC = 20^{\circ} + 50^{\circ} = 70^{\circ}$

Find side $AD$: $\sin(70^{\circ})=\displaystyle\frac{AD}{12.5}$, $AD = 12.5 \times \sin(70^{\circ})$, $AD=11.7$ cm

Find side $BD$: $\sin(20^{\circ})=\displaystyle\frac{BD}{12.5}$, $BD = 12.5 \times \sin(20^{\circ})$, $BD=4.3$ cm

Find side $DC$: $\tan(35^{\circ})=\displaystyle\frac{11.7\dots}{DC}$, $DC=\displaystyle\frac{11.7\ldots}{\tan(35^{\circ})}$, $DC=16.8$ cm

Find side $b$: $11.7\ldots^2 + 16.7\ldots^2 = b^2$, $b=\sqrt{11.7\ldots^2 + 16.7\ldots^2}$, $b=20.5$ cm

Find side $a$: $BD + DC = 11.7\ldots + 16.7\ldots = 28.5$ cm
