Carbon dioxide (CO₂) emissions from human activities are now higher than at any point in our history. Recent data reveals that global CO₂ emissions were 150 times higher in 2011 than they were in 1850. The global CO₂ emissions between 1850 and 2014 can be modelled with the following exponential function $e(t) = 260*1.0303^t$ where $t$ is the year since 1850 and $e(t)$ is the global CO₂ emissions in megatonnes.

  1. Sketch the function, label the axes and indicate your window settings.
  2. What year did the global CO₂ emisions exceed 1 billion tonnes?
  3. State a possible domain and range for the exponential function in this context.
