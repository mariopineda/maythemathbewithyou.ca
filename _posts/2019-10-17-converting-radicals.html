Express as a mixed radical in simplest form.

  1. $\sqrt{20}$
  2. $\sqrt{300}$
  3. $\sqrt{99}$
  4. $\sqrt[3]{7000}$

Express as an entire radical.

  1. $7\sqrt{10}$
  2. $2\sqrt{13}$
  3. $6\sqrt{2}$
  4. $3\sqrt[3]{-2}$

Express as a mixed radical in simplest form.

  1. $\sqrt{20}$ $=\sqrt{4 \times 5}$ $= \sqrt{4}\sqrt{5}$ $=2\sqrt{5}$
  2. $\sqrt{300}$ $=\sqrt{100 \times 3}$ $= \sqrt{100}\sqrt{3}$ $=10\sqrt{3}$
  3. $\sqrt{99}$ $=\sqrt{9 \times 11}$ $= 3\sqrt{11}$
  4. $\sqrt[3]{7000}$ $=\sqrt[3]{1000 \times 7}$ $= 10\sqrt[3]{7}$

Express as an entire radical.

  1. $7\sqrt{10}$
  2. $2\sqrt{13}$
  3. $6\sqrt{2}$
  4. $3\sqrt[3]{-2}$
