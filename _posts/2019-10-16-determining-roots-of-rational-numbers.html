  1. Evaluate $\sqrt{10}$ to the nearest hundredth.
  2. Evaluate $\displaystyle\frac{1}{2} \sqrt[3]{-81}$ to the nearest tenth.
  3. Evaluate $-\displaystyle\frac{3}{2} \sqrt[9]{-527}$ to the nearest hundredth.
  4. Express $\sqrt{35}$ as a product of radicals.
  5. A square as an area of 225 cm$^2$. What is the radius, to the nearest tenth of a mm, of the largest circle that can fit inside the square?
  6. Determine the side length of a cube that has a volume of 720 mm$^3$.
