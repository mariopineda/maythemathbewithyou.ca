  1. Determine a simplified expression for the area of the shaded region.
  2. Determine a simplified expression for the perimeter of the shaded region.

A.

$$ \begin{align} &= (2x+1)(3x-2)-x^2-x^2-x^2-x^2 \\ &= (2x+1)(3x-2)-4x^2\\ &= 6x^2-4x+3x-2-4x^2 \\ &= \boxed{2x^2+x-2} \end{align} $$

B.

$$ \begin{align} &= (2x+1)+(3x-2)+(2x+1)+(3x-2) \\ &= 2x+1+3x-2+2x+1+3x-2 \\ &= \boxed{10x-2} \end{align} $$
