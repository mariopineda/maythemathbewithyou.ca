Two dice are rolled and the numbers on each die are multiplied together.

  1. Create an outcome table for the sample space of products?
  2. What is the $P(\mbox{product is even number})$?
  3. What is the $P(\mbox{product is odd number})'$?
  4. What is $P(\mbox{product is} \leq 10)?$
  5. What is $P(\mbox{product is} > 10)?$
  6. What is $P(\mbox{product is an even number} < 20)'$?
