Simplify the following radicals.

  1. $(5\sqrt{18})(2\sqrt{6})$
  2. $\sqrt{5}(4\sqrt{5}-\sqrt{15}+2\sqrt{3})$
  3. $\sqrt{a}(\sqrt{a}-4\sqrt{b})$
  4. $\displaystyle\frac{3\sqrt{80}}{2\sqrt{2}}$
  5. $\displaystyle\frac{8\sqrt{39} + 8\sqrt{75}}{4\sqrt{4}}$
  6. $\displaystyle\frac{20\sqrt{12}}{12\sqrt{20}}$

{% if true %}

A.

\[ \begin{align} & 5\sqrt{18})(2\sqrt{6}) \\ = & 5 \times 2 \sqrt{18 \times 6} \\ = & 10 \sqrt{108} \\ = & 10 \sqrt{36 \times 3} \\ = & 10 \times 6 \sqrt{3} \\ = & 60\sqrt{3} \end{align} \]

B.

\[ \begin{align} & \sqrt{5}(4\sqrt{5}-\sqrt{15}+2\sqrt{3}) \\ = & 4\sqrt{5}\sqrt{5} - \sqrt{15}\sqrt+{5} + 2\sqrt{3}\sqrt{5} \\ = & 4 \times 5 - \sqrt{75} + 2\sqrt{15} \\ = & 20 - \sqrt{25 \times 3} + 2\sqrt{15} \\ = & 20 - 5\sqrt{3} + 2\sqrt{15} \end{align} \]

C.

\[ \begin{align} & \sqrt{a}(\sqrt{a}-4\sqrt{b}) \\ = & \sqrt{a}\sqrt{a} - 4\sqrt{b}\sqrt{a} \\ = & a - 4\sqrt{ab} \end{align} \]

D.

\[ \begin{align} & \displaystyle\frac{3\sqrt{80}}{2\sqrt{2}} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{\displaystyle\frac{80}{2}} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{3}{3} \sqrt{40} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{3}{2} \sqrt{4 \times 10} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{3}{2} \times 2 \sqrt{10} \\ = & 4\sqrt{10} \end{align} \]

E.

\[ \begin{align} & \displaystyle\frac{8\sqrt{39} + 8\sqrt{75}}{4\sqrt{4}} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{8\sqrt{39}}{4\sqrt{4}} + \displaystyle\frac{8\sqrt{75}}{4\sqrt{4}} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{2\sqrt{39}}{2} + \displaystyle\frac{2\sqrt{25 \times 3}}{2} \\ = & \sqrt{39} + 5\sqrt{3} \end{align} \]

F.

\[ \begin{align} & \displaystyle\frac{20\sqrt{12}}{12\sqrt{20}} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{5\sqrt{4 \times 3}}{3\sqrt{4 \times 5}} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{5 \times 2\sqrt{3}}{3 \times 2\sqrt{5}} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{5\sqrt{3}}{3\sqrt{5}} \end{align} \]
