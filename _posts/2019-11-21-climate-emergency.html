You are making numbers using only the digits 0, 1, 2, 3 and 4. no digits are repeated.

  1. How many 5-digit natural numers can you make?
  2. How many natural numbers can you make consisting of at least 4 digits?
  3. How many odd natural numbers can you make consisting of at least 4 digits?
  4. How many 5-natural numbers can you make that are divisible by 5?
