  1. Sparky the dog is tied to a pole in the middle of the yard using a 8 ft leash. Sparky has ruined the grass in the area that his leash has allowed him access to. This area will need to be reseeded. Grass seed costs \$2.50 per package and one package covers 50 ft$^2$. What will it cost to reseed the yard?
  2. The surface area of a cube is 70 cm$^2$. Determine the volume of the cube, to the nearest tenth.

A.

  1. The area of the yard that Sparky has access is a circle with a 8 ft radius. The area of this circle is $A=\pi r^2 = \pi 8^2=201.06...$ ft$^2$.
  2. Each package of seed covers 50 ft$^2$ so the number of packages required to seed the damaged area is $201.06.../50 = 4.02... \approx 4$ packages.
  3. Each package costs \$2.50 so the total cost of $4.02...$ packages will be $2.50 \times 4.02...=10.05...\approx 10$ dollars.

B.

  1. The formula for calculating the suface area of a cube is $SA=6x^2$, since we know $SA=70$ cm$^2$ we can calculate the side length of the cube by substituting in the surface area and solve for $x$, $70=6x^2 \Rightarrow \frac{\displaystyle 70}{\displaystyle 6} = x^2 \Rightarrow x = \sqrt{\frac{\displaystyle 70}{\displaystyle 6}} \Rightarrow x = 3.41...$
