A store sells a case of a dozen water bottles for $8.50 and indidual bottles of the same brand of water for $1.55.

  • A. How much does each bottle of water cin the case of 12 cost? Round your answer to the nearest tenth.
  • How much would you save by buying a case of water, rather than 12 individual bottles?
  • The unit price for a bottle is $\frac{cost for the case}{number of bottles} = \frac{8.50}{12} = 0.708\bar{3} \approx 0.71$. Each bottle costs $0.71.
