Factor:

  1. Factor $x^2+y-x-xy$
  2. Factor $36a^2-121d^2$
  3. Factor $(3x^3-27x)(x^2-9)$
  4. The volume of a rectangular prism is $V(x)=10x^3-25x^2-60. Determine the expressions for the length, width and height of the prism.$

A.

$a=1 \Rightarrow$ Factor by Method of Inspection. $b=10$ and $c=24 \Rightarrow 6+4=b=10$ and $6 \times 4=c=24$, hence $x^2+10x+24 = \boxed{(x+6)(x+4)}$

B.

$a \neq 1 \Rightarrow$ Factor by Method of Decomposition. $b=7$ and $a \times c = 2 \times 6=12 \Rightarrow 3+4=b=7$ and $4 \times 4 = a \times c=12 \Rightarrow$ decompose x-term $$ \begin{align} &= 2x^2+4x+3x+6 \\ &= 2x(x+2)+3(x+2) \\ &= \boxed{(2x+3)(x+2)} \end{align} $$

C.

GCF=$3a \Rightarrow 3a(3a^2-13a+14)$. $a \neq 1 \Rightarrow$ Factor by Method of Decomposition. $b=-13$ and $a \times c=3 \times 14=42 \Rightarrow -6+(-7)=b=-13$ and $(-6)(-7)=a \times c=42 \Rightarrow$ decompose x-term. $$ \begin{align} &= 3a(3a^2-6a-74+14) \\ &= 3a[3a(a-2)-7(a-2)] \\ &= \boxed{3a[(3a-7)(a-2)]} \end{align} $$
