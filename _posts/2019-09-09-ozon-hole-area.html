The ozone layer is a region of Earth's atmosphere that absorbs most of the Sun's harmful ultraviolet radiation. Certain human-made chemicals like chlorofluorocarbons (CFC) create a hole in the ozone layer over Antarctica each spring. The size of the ozon hole between the years 1979 and 2018 can be modelled by the cubuc function \[a=0.001787t^3-0.1324t^2+3.1576t-0.8924\] where \[t\] is the year since 1979 and \[a\] is the average area of the ozon hole in millions of square kilometers.

  1. Sketch the function describing the area of the ozon hole. Label the axes appropriatelly. What are your window settings?
  2. Determine the size of the ozon hole in 1979 and in 2018. How many times larger was the area in 2018 compared to 1979?
  3. What is an appropriate domain and range for this context? Round quantities to the nearest thousand if necessary
