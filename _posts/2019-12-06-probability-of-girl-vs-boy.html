A 2015 study of sex ratios of newborn reported that for women between below the age of 30 the probability of giving birth to a boy is 0.52. Draw the sample space. Determine the probability for a woman with four children before the age of 30 to have...

  1. 3 girls and 1 boy.
  2. at least 2 girls.
  3. at most 3 boys.
  4. not having exactly 3 girls.
