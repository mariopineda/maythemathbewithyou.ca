Consider the following pattern of multiplications: $$123456789 \times 9 \times 1 = 111111111$$ $$123456789 \times 9 \times 2 = 222222222$$ $$123456789 \times 9 \times 3 = 333333333$$

  1. Use the number pattern to make a conjecture.
  2. Show two more examples that support your conjecture.
  3. Provide a counterexample to show your conjecture is false.

A.

When the number 123456789 is multiplied by 9 and then by a whole number $n$, the product is $nnnnnnnnn$.

B.

For example: $123456789 \times 9 \times 5 = 555555555$, $123456789 \times 9 \times 6 = 666666666$

C.

For example, $123456789 \times 9 \times 10 = 1111111110$
