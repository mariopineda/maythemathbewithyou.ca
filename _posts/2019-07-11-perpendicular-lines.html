  1. Given a line segment with slope $2$ and points $E(-4,9)$ and $F(x,-5)$, determine the value of missing coordinate to the nearest tenth.
  2. Given the line $AB$ with endpoints $A(3,5)$ and $B(0,-2)$ and line $CD$ with end points $C(5,3)$ and $D(3,2)$, determine whether lines $AB$ and $CD$ are parallel, perpendicular or neither.

A.

$$ \begin{array}{rcll} m_{EF} & = & \frac{\displaystyle{y_F-y_E}}{\displaystyle{x_F-x_E}} & \mbox{Slope formula} \\ 2 & = & \frac{\displaystyle{-5-9}}{\displaystyle{x-(-4)}} & \mbox{Substitute in know values} \\ 2 & = & \frac{\displaystyle -14}{\displaystyle x+4} & \mbox{Simplify} \\ (x+4) \times 2 & = & \frac{\displaystyle -14}{\displaystyle x+4}(x+4) & \mbox{Multiply both sides by} x+4 \\ 2x+4 & = & -14 & \mbox{Simplify} \\ 2x & = & -22 & \mbox{Subtract 4 from both sides} \\ x & = & -11 & \mbox{Divide both sides by 2} \end{array} $$

B.

Determine the slopes of the two line segments, $m_{AB}$ and $m_{CD}$. $$ \begin{array}{rcl} m_{AB} & = & \frac{\displaystyle y_B-y_A}{\displaystyle x_B-X_A} \\ m_{AB} & = & \frac{\displaystyle -2-5}{\displaystyle 0-3} \\ m_{AB} & = & \frac{\displaystyle -7}{\displaystyle -3} = \frac{\displaystyle 7}{\displaystyle 3} m_{CD} & = & \frac{\displaystyle y_D-y_C}{\displaystyle x_D-x_C} \\ m_{CD} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 2-3}{\displaystyle 3-5} \\ m_{CD} & = & \frac{\displaystyle -1}{\displaystyle -2} = \frac{\displaystyle 1}{\displaystyle 2} \\ \end{array} $$
