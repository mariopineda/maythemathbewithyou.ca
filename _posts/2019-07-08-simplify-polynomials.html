  1. $$ (3-a-2a^2)+(9-4a+5a^2) $$
  2. $$ (5x^2-8x+3)-(3x^3-3x-1) $$
  3. Subtract the sum of $ 2x^3-7x^2-6x+1 $ and $ 8-3x+5x^2-4x^3 $ from $ 2x^3-7x+9 $.
  1. $$(3-a-2a^2)+(9-4a+5a^2) = 3-a-2a^2+9-4a+5a^2 = \boxed{3a^2-5a+12}$$
  2. $$(5x^2-8x+3)-(3x^3-3x-1) = 5x^2-8x+3-3x^3+3x+1 = -3x^3+5x^2-5x+4$$
  3. $$(2x^3-7x+9)-((2x^3-7x^2-6x+1)+(8-3x+5x^2-4x^3)) = (2x^3-7x+9)-(2x^3-7x^2-6x+1+8-3x+5x^2-4x^3) = 2x^3-7x+9-2x^3+7x^2+6x-1-8+3x-5x^2+4x^3 = 4x^3+2x^2+2x$$
