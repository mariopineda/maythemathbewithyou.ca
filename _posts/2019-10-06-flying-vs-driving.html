An airliner with 100 passengers flying between Edmonton and Vancouver emits 13 tonnes of CO2. Driving an average SUV between Edmonton and Vancouver emits 614 kg of CO2. Find the unit rate of CO2 emissions per person for the two modes of transportation. Which mode of transportation has the lowest emissions for a family of four travelling between Edmonton and Vancouver? 1 tonnes = 1000 kg.

  3. $\displaystyle\frac{a+3}{(a+4)(a-5)}, a \neq -4,5$, $a+4=0 \Rightarrow a=-4$, $a-5=0 \Rightarrow a=5$

  5. $\displaystyle\frac{d}{d^4-81}, d=\pm 3$, factor denominator as a Differece of Squares $(d^4-81) = (d^2-9)(d^2+9)$, factor first binomial as a Difference of Squares $(d^2-9) = (d-3)(d+3)$
  6. $\displaystyle\frac{x-1}{x^2+1}$ (no NPV)
