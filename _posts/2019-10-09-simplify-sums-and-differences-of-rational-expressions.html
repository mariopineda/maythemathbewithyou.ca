Simplify the following rational expressions.

  1. $\displaystyle\frac{1}{a+1} + \displaystyle\frac{1}{a-1}$
  2. $\displaystyle\frac{p-1}{p+2} + \displaystyle\frac{p+2}{p+3}$
  3. $\displaystyle\frac{5h+1}{m(m-7)} + \displaystyle\frac{4m+8}{m(7-m)}$
  4. $\displaystyle\frac{5}{x+2} - \displaystyle\frac{2}{x+5}$
  5. $\displaystyle\frac{3y-1}{3y-2} - \displaystyle\frac{3y-2}{3y+1}$
  6. $\displaystyle\frac{3}{25-a^2} - \displaystyle\frac{a+5}{2a-10}$
{% if false %}

A.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{x^2-9}{6x+24} \times \displaystyle\frac{10x+40}{x(x+3)} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{(x+3)(x-3)}{6(x+4)} \times \displaystyle\frac{10(x+4)}{x(x+3)} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{x-3}{6} \times \displaystyle\frac{10}{x} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{x-3}{3} \times \displaystyle\frac{5}{x} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{5(x-3)}{3x}, x \neq -4, -3, 0 \end{array} $$

B.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{x-5}{2x} \times \displaystyle\frac{4x^2}{2x-10} \\ =\displaystyle\frac{x-5}{2x} \times \displaystyle\frac{2x}{2} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2x}{2} \\ = x, \neq 0,5 \end{array} $$

C.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{m-8}{m^2} \times 2m^2 \\ = 2(m-8), m \neq 0 \end{array} $$

D.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{6x^2}{15} \div \displaystyle\frac{12x}{30x^3} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{6x^2}{15} \times \displaystyle\frac{30x^3}{12x} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{x}{1} \times \displaystyle\frac{2x^3}{2} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2x^4}{2} \\ = x^4, x \neq 0 \end{array} $$

E.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{3b+9}{b+3} \div \displaystyle\frac{b}{b} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{3b+9}{b+3} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{3(b+3)}{b+3} \\ = 3, b \neq -3, 0 \end{array} $$

F.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{2y^3-4y^2}{3y^2-9y} \div \displaystyle\frac{y^2-4}{(y+2)(y-3)} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2y^3-4y^2}{3y^2-9y} \times \displaystyle\frac{(y+2)(y-3)}{y^2-4} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2y^2(y-2)}{3y(y-3)} \times \displaystyle\frac{(y+2)(y-3)}{(y+2)(y-2)} \\ = 2y, y \neq -2,0,2,3 \end{array} $$
