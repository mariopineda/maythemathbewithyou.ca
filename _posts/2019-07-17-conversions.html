Convert the following unit as indicated, rounded to the nearest hundredth when applicable. For metric units, provide answer in decimal and in scientific notation when above or below $\times 1000$ the base unit (i.e. if you answer has three or more leading or trailing zeros).

  1. $1140 \mbox{ m} \Rightarrow \mbox{km}$
  2. $0.0611 \mbox{ m} \Rightarrow \mbox{dm}$
  3. $0.000075 \mbox{ L} \Rightarrow \mbox{mL}$
  4. $640000 \mbox{ m} \Rightarrow \mbox{hm}$
  5. $90 \mbox{ in} \Rightarrow \mbox{ft and in}$
  6. $0.005 \mbox{ mi} \Rightarrow \mbox{yd and feet and in}$

All conversions are done using proportional reasoning.

A.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ km}}{\displaystyle 1140 \mbox{ m}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ km}}{\displaystyle 1000 \mbox{ m}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1}{\displaystyle 1000} \times 1140 \\ x & = & 1.140 \mbox{ km} \end{array} $$

B.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ dm}}{\displaystyle 0.0611 \mbox{ m}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 10 \mbox{ dm}}{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ m}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 10}{\displaystyle 1} \times 0.0611 \\ x & = & 0.611 \mbox{ dm} \end{array} $$

C.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ mL}}{\displaystyle 0.000075 \mbox{ L}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1000 \mbox{ mL}}{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ L}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1000}{\displaystyle 1} \times 0.000075 \\ x & = & 0.075 \mbox{ mL} \end{array} $$

D.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ hm}}{\displaystyle 640000 \mbox{ m}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ hm}}{\displaystyle 100 \mbox{ m}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1}{\displaystyle 100} \times 640000 \\ x & = & 6400 \mbox{ hm} = 6.4 \times 10^3 \mbox{ hm} \end{array} $$

E.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ ft}}{\displaystyle 90 \mbox{ in}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ ft}}{\displaystyle 12 \mbox{ in}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1}{\displaystyle 12} \times 90 \\ x & = & 7.5 \mbox{ ft} \mbox{ (Convert decimal part to inches)} \\ \frac{\displaystyle y \mbox{ in}}{\displaystyle 0.5 \mbox{ ft}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 12 \mbox{ in}}{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ ft}} \\ y & = & \frac{\displaystyle 12}{\displaystyle 1} \times 0.5 \\ y & = & 6 \\ \end{array} $$ Solution: 7 ft and 6 in.

F.
