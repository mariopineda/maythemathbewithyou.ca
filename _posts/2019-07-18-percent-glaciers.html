Due to human caused global warming glaciers in the Rockies have been shrinking in size in size faster in the last 150 years than they have since the last great ice age ended 10,000 years ago. Between 1985 and 2005, Jasper National Park lost 135 of its 554 glaciers and Banff National Park lost 29 of its 365. Determine, to the nearest tenth, the percentage of glacier each park lost during these two decades.

Percent of glacier lost in Banff National Park between 1985-2005: $$ \displaystyle\frac{29}{365} \times 100 = 7.94520... \approx 7.9 \% $$
