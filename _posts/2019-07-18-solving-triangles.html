  1. Draw $\triangle ABC$ with $\angle B=27^\circ$, $a=10 \mbox{ cm}$, $\angle A=90^\circ$.
  2. Determine length of $c$ to the nearest tenth.
  3. Determine $\angle C$ to the nearest whole degree.

All conversions are done using proportional reasoning.

A.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ km}}{\displaystyle 1140 \mbox{ m}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ km}}{\displaystyle 1000 \mbox{ m}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1}{\displaystyle 1000} \times 1140 \\ x & = & 1.140 \mbox{ km} \approx 1.14 \mbox{ km} \end{array} $$

B.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ dm}}{\displaystyle 0.0611 \mbox{ m}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 10 \mbox{ dm}}{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ m}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 10}{\displaystyle 1} \times 0.0611 \\ x & = & 0.611 \mbox{ dm} \approx 0.61 \mbox{ dm} \end{array} $$

C.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ mL}}{\displaystyle 0.000075 \mbox{ L}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1000 \mbox{ mL}}{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ L}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1000}{\displaystyle 1} \times 0.000075 \\ x & = & 0.075 \mbox{ mL} \approx 0.07 \mbox{ mL} \end{array} $$

D.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ hm}}{\displaystyle 640000 \mbox{ m}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ hm}}{\displaystyle 100 \mbox{ m}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1}{\displaystyle 100} \times 640000 \\ x & = & 6400 \mbox{ hm} = 6.4 \times 10^3 \mbox{ hm} \end{array} $$

E.

$$ \begin{array}{rcl} \frac{\displaystyle x \mbox{ ft}}{\displaystyle 90 \mbox{ in}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ ft}}{\displaystyle 12 \mbox{ in}} \\ x & = & \frac{\displaystyle 1}{\displaystyle 12} \times 90 \\ x & = & 7.5 \mbox{ ft} \mbox{ (Convert decimal part to inches)} \\ \frac{\displaystyle y \mbox{ in}}{\displaystyle 0.5 \mbox{ ft}} & = & \frac{\displaystyle 12 \mbox{ in}}{\displaystyle 1 \mbox{ ft}} \\ y & = & \frac{\displaystyle 12}{\displaystyle 1} \times 0.5 \\ y & = & 6 \mbox{ in}\\ \end{array} $$ Solution: 7 ft and 6 in.
