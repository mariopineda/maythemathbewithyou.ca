Consider the following pattern of multiplications: $$ 123456789 \times 9 \times 1 = 111111111 $$ $$ 123456789 \times 9 \times 1 = 111111111 $$

Percent of glacier lost in Jasper National Park between 1985-2005: $$ \displaystyle\frac{135}{554} \times 100 = 24.3682... \approx 24.4 \% $$ Percent of glacier lost in Banff National Park between 1985-2005: $$ \displaystyle\frac{29}{365} \times 100 = 7.94520... \approx 7.9 \% $$ During these two decades Jasper National Park lost a larger percentage of glaciers than Banff National Park. In 2005 Jasper National Park had 419 glaciers left ($$554-135$$) and Banff National Park had 336 glaciers left ($$365-29$$).
